About Us

Taudol Pariwar Samaj is a community organization formed by the residents of Taudol village to promote unity, preserve cultural heritage, and support one another. Our society has a long-standing history of cooperation, celebrating traditions, and building a strong network of families both within and outside the village.

We aim to organize events, assist our members, and maintain a platform where all members can stay connected and updated about important happenings.
